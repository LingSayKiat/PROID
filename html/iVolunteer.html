Welcome to iVolunteer

iVolunteer is an app designed to encourage family volunteering as a routine part of life in Singapore, making it appealing across generations.

About iVolunteer

Our mission is to build a more inclusive and caring society through giving. iVolunteer offers a platform to make family volunteering easy, engaging, and rewarding. By integrating an interactive app, social media campaigns, and interactive displays, iVolunteer aims to foster community bonds and promote intergenerational volunteerism across Singapore.
