Welcome to iVolunteer

iVolunteer is an app designed to encourage family volunteering as a routine part of life in Singapore. Our platform makes it easy, engaging, and rewarding for families to volunteer together, fostering community bonds and promoting intergenerational volunteerism.

Join us in making a difference. Together, we can build a more inclusive and caring society through the power of family volunteering.

Features

Flexible Scheduling

Find and schedule volunteering activities to fit families’ busy schedules with our user-friendly app.

As shown in the above UI in our app, you are able to apply for and attend adhoc events that can fit into your own timing and schedule.

Social Media Campaigns

Engaging content to inspire and educate about the benefits of volunteering. Follow us on Facebook, Instagram, Telegram and X.

We are on multiple social media platforms. Do join our Telegram channel to get the latest updates on what we are doing.

DIY Volunteer Kits

Flexible participation for families to engage in meaningful activities at their convenience. Kits include all necessary materials and instructions.

Rewards

Vouchers, badges, certificates, and leaderboards to make volunteering fun and rewarding. Track your progress and compete with others.

The Rewards section provides additional information about how you can earn and redeem vouchers, badges, and certificates through volunteering. Track your progress on leaderboards and compete with others to make volunteering more engaging and rewarding.
